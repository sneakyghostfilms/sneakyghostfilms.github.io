    About the Founder

    Scott Danzig, a software engineer by trade, has been enthralled with the challenge of filmmaking for most of his life, and has been making short films since he moved to New York in 2010. He is the former organizer of the all-volunteer meetup.com based filmmaking group, Dark Kidney Films. He gained a great deal of experience, but aimed to take his filmmaking to the next level, working with those he feels best suited for bringing his vision into a reality.

    Although he is very open-minded with experimentation, he is most inspired by the darker genre of films encompassing eerie worlds, lost spirits, and frightening situations.
